Welcome to "Othello Master" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

The App itself does not directly collect your personal information. However, the App uses Google AdMob to provide advertising services, which may collect the following information:

  • Device performance and diagnostic data

2. Use of Information

The above information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving advertising services
  • Advertising performance analysis
  • App performance optimization

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display rewarded ads. For more details, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Game progress (such as records of defeated opponents) is stored only on your local device and is not uploaded to any server.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically designed for children under 13. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
